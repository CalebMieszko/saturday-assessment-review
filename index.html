Before Sunrise

1995

This is a fun tribute page to Before Sunrise, my favorite movie!

Before Sunrise is one of my wife and I's favorite movies, we watched it for the first time right around the day we got married. We watched it again when we renewed our vows. It's about a couple that meet for one night only while on a trip abroad and fall in love for decades, though they don't see each other again for a very long time.
